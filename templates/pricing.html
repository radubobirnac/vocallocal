Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated.

What happens if I exceed my limits?

Basic and Professional users can enable Pay-As-You-Go to continue using services beyond their monthly limits. You'll only be charged for the additional usage at transparent per-unit rates. Free users need to upgrade to access this feature.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Our Free Plan gives you access to basic features with generous limits to try out the platform before upgrading.

How does Pay-As-You-Go work?

Pay-As-You-Go is available for Basic and Professional subscribers. When you exceed your monthly plan limits, you can continue using services with transparent per-unit billing. Each service is tracked independently, so you only pay for the specific services you use beyond your plan allowances.
